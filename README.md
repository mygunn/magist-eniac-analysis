# Magist & Eniac – Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer reviews and delivery performance to evaluate whether Eniac should partner with Magist for expansion into the Brazilian market.

## 🎯 Objective
The goal is to identify potential risks in customer satisfaction and delivery operations, especially for high-end tech products.

## 📊 Key Insights
- Customer satisfaction is generally high, with most reviews being positive.
- Non-tech products have significantly higher customer activity than tech products.
- Delayed orders have a much higher negative review rate (~55% vs ~9%).
- Even though delays are relatively rare, they drive a large share of customer dissatisfaction.
- Negative reviews are mainly related to delivery issues, not product quality.

## ⚠️ Business Impact
- Delivery performance appears strong on the surface (~90% on time).
- However, operational issues (delays, missing items, poor service) create risk.
- This is especially critical for high-value tech products.

## 📈 Conclusion
Although Magist offers a scalable solution, the data suggests potential risks in delivery quality and customer experience.

Therefore, a full-scale partnership may not be recommended without improvements in logistics and service quality.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- SQL
- Tableau
- Data Analysis techniques

## 📁 Dataset
The analysis is based on a dataset provided for evaluating Magist’s performance.
